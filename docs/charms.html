About charms

The Charms bars is a systems-wide toolbars that houses for important services. The Charms bars is a vertical or horizontal toolbar, found on the: right-side, left-side, top-side or bottom-side of the screen.

To create charms add attribute data-role="charms" to block element and setup charms position with attribute data-position="right|left|top|bottom".
